11.2.29.080 Groundwater Quality Standards

(a) General Standard. All groundwater shall be free from toxic, radioactive, conventional, non-conventional, deleterious, or other polluting substances in concentrations that exceed naturally occurring background levels.

(b) Protection of Water Supply. All groundwater shall maintain a level of water quality that provides for use of the water as a drinking water source.

(c) Numeric Standards for Regional Aquifer. All groundwater in the regional aquifer shall not contain analytes at concentrations exceeding those established in Table I. All other groundwater shall meet the general standard in Section 11.2.29.080(a).

(d) Alternative and Future Standards. The DECP may recommend to the Governor and Council the adoption of alternative or additional numeric groundwater quality standards, including standards for additional aquifers and for substances not identified in this Act, based on new data, updated investigations, risk assessment, protection of public health, cultural uses, or other relevant scientific or regulatory considerations.

(June 12, 2026, SI-R26-006.)
