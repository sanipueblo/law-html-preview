Resolution No. SI-R19-002: Authorizing the 2019-2022 Contracting of the Indian Child Welfare Act (ICWA) Program Through Public Law 93-638

At a duly called meeting of the Council of San Ildefonso Pueblo on the 30th day of January, 2019, the following Resolution was adopted:

New Section 6.5.21.010

WHEREAS, the Pueblo de San Ildefonso ("Pueblo") is a sovereign and self-governing Tribe, recognized as such by the United States of America; and

WHEREAS, the Council is authorized to make laws for the Pueblo de San Ildefonso pursuant to Section 5 of the Agreement, the governing document of the pueblo; and

WHEREAS, the Pueblo, by and through United States Public Law 93-638, has contracted and administered key functions for assistance to the Tribe through the Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) to include the Indian Child Welfare Act (ICWA) Program; and

WHEREAS, the Indian Child Welfare Act (ICWA) Program, receives funding from the BIA to administer the ICWA Program in compliance with the respective scope of work; and

WHEREAS, the Pueblo wishes to continue contracting ICWA for a four (4)-year period, 2019 through 2022.

New Section 6.5.21.020

NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED the Pueblo de San Ildefonso Council hereby approves submission of the Application for Contract for the Indian Child Welfare Act (ICWA) Program for a four-year term (2019-2022); and

New Section 6.5.21.030

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, the Pueblo de San Ildefonso wishes to enter into a Public Law 93-638 contract for these funds, and

New Section 6.5.21.040

BE IT FINALLY RESOLVED, that the Pueblo de San Ildefonso Council hereby approves and adopts this resolution in accordance with the requirements of the Agreement, according to the published Council procedures, and authorizes and directs the Governor, or his designee, to take such actions as deemed necessary to carry out the intent of this resolution.

CERTIFICATION

The foregoing resolution was duly voted upon by the Council on January 30 , 2019, at a meeting with a vote of 9 in favor, 0 opposed, 0 abstaining and 1 absent (Governor presiding and not voting) pursuant to the authority vested in the Council by the Agreement of the Pueblo de San Ildefonso.

Irene Tse-Pe, Council Secretary
