Resolution No. SI-R20-002: Authorizing Application to the Local Government Planning Fund and Designating the Highest Elected Official/Authorized Officer, Primary Contact, and Consultant

At a duly called meeting of the Council of San Ildefonso Pueblo on the 12th day of March, 2020, the following Resolution was adopted:

WHEREAS, the Pueblo administers programs impacting economic development, housing, education, environmental quality, infrastructure, and other local government needs and services; and,

WHEREAS, the New Mexico Finance Authority ('NMFA") has the Local Government Planning Fund ("LGPF") to assist qualified entities, including pueblos located in New Mexico, with water and wastewater projects, which include master plans, conservation plans, economic development plans, infrastructure plans, and energy efficiency audits; and,

WHEREAS, to promote economic development on the Pueblo, the Pueblo plans to submit an application for the LGPF to the NMFA requesting $50,000 to update the 2008 Los Alamos National Labs warehouse study ("Project"); and,

WHEREAS, when applying for and/or administering the LGPF, the Council desires to designate the Pueblo's Governor, as the Highest Elected Official/Authorized Officer, the Tribal Administrator as the Primary Contact, and San Ildefonso Services, LLC, a limited liability company wholly owned by the Pueblo, as the Consultant.

NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Council hereby enacts the attached law entitled, "AUTHORIZING APPLICATION TO THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT PLANNING FUND AND DESIGNATING THE HIGHEST ELECTED OFFICIAL/AUTHORIZED OFFICER, PRIMARY CONTACT, AND CONSULTANT."

CERTIFICATION

The foregoing resolution was duly voted upon by the Council on March 12, 2020, at a meeting with a vote of 8 in favor, 0 opposed, 1 abstaining and 1 absent (Governor presiding and not voting) pursuant to the authority vested in the Council by the Agreement of the Pueblo de San Ildefonso.

William S. Christian, Council Secretary

AUTHORIZING APPLICATION TO THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT PLANNING FUND AND DESIGNATING THE HIGHEST ELECTED OFFICIAL/AUTHORIZED OFFICER, PRIMARY CONTACT, AND CONSULTANT

New Section 6.5.26.010

Section 1.1. Short Title

This enactment shall be known as the Act, "AUTHORIZING APPLICATION TO THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT PLANNING FUND AND DESIGNATING THE HIGHEST ELECTED OFFICIAL/AUTHORIZED OFFICER, PRIMARY CONTACT, AND CONSULTANT ("Act").

New Section 6.5.26.020

Section 1.2. Definitions

(a) "Council" means the Pueblo de San Ildefonso Council.

(b) "LGPF" means Local Government Planning Fund.

(c) "NMFA" means New Mexico Finance Authority.

(d) "Project" means update to the 2008 Los Alamos National Labs warehouse study.

(e) "Pueblo" means the Pueblo de San Ildefonso.

New Section 6.5.26.030

Section 1.3. Purpose

The purpose of this Act is to authorize the Pueblo to apply for the LGPF in the amount of $50,000 from the NMFA for the project and to designate the Highest Elected Official/Authorized Officer, Primary Contact, and Consultant when applying for and/or administering the LGPF.

New Section 6.5.26.040

Section 1.4. Findings

The Council hereby finds that:

(a) The Pueblo administers programs impacting economic development, housing, education, environmental quality, infrastructure, and other local government needs and services; and

(b) The NMFA has the LGPF to assist qualified entities, including pueblos located in New Mexico with water and wastewater projects, which include master plans, conservation plans, economic development plans, infrastructure plans, and energy efficiency audits; and

(c) To promote economic development on the Pueblo, the Pueblo plans to submit an application for the LGPF to the NMFA requesting $50,000 to assist with the Project; and

(d) When applying for and/or administering the LGPF, the Council desires to designate the Pueblo's Governor as the Highest Elected Official/Authorized Officer, the Tribal liability company wholly owned by the Pueblo, as the Consultant.

New Section 6.5.26.050

Section 1.5. Authorizations

(a) The Council authorizes the Pueblo to apply for the LGPF in the amount of $50,000 from the NMFA for the Project; and

(b) When applying for and/or administering the LGPF, the Council desires to designate the Pueblo's Governor as the Highest Elected Official/Authorized Officer, the Tribal liability company wholly owned by the Pueblo, as the Consultant.

(c) The Governor or his designee is authorized to sign such documents or take such actions as necessary to carry out the intent of this authorization.
