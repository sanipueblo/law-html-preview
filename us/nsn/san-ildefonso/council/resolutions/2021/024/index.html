Resolution No. SI-R21-024: Personnel Policy Manual Approval Act of 2021

At a duly called meeting of the Council of San Ildefonso Pueblo on the 28th day of October, 2021, the following law was adopted:

WHEREAS, in 2012, the Council reaffirmed the Personnel Policy Manual that had existed for many years to govern the employment practices of the Pueblo; and,

WHEREAS, in 2017, the Council enacted the Personnel Policy Manual Clarification Act to, again, reaffirm the existing Personnel Policy Manual, and to clarify that the Council is vested with the authority to approve any future modifications to the Manual and that the Governor is prohibited from making changes to the Manual by Executive Order; and,

WHEREAS, the Governor and Council have identified the need to make improvements to the Personnel Policy Manual, and over the course of several years, proposed modifications to the Manual have been developed, circulated, and reviewed by multiple officials of the Pueblo.

NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Council hereby enacts the attached law entitled "PERSONNEL POLICY MANUAL APPROVAL ACT OF 2021."

CERTIFICATION

The foregoing resolution was duly voted upon by the Council on October 28, 2021, at a meeting with a vote of 9 in favor, 0 opposed, 0 abstaining and 1 absent (Governor presiding and not voting) pursuant to the authority vested in the Council by the Agreement of the Pueblo de San Ildefonso.

Terrence K. Garcia, Council Secretary

PERSONNEL POLICY MANUAL APPROVAL ACT OF 2021

New Section 6.7.1.070

Section 1.1. Short Title

This enactment shall be known as the "PERSONNEL POLICY MANUAL APPROVAL ACT OF 2021."

New Section 6.7.1.080

Section 1.2. Purpose

The purpose of this Act is to approve amendments to the Personnel Policy Manual.

New Section 6.7.1.090

Section 1.3. Findings

The Council hereby finds and declares that:

(a) In 2012, the Council reaffirmed the Personnel Policy Manual that had existed for many years to govern the employment practices of the Pueblo; and

(b) In 2017, the Council enacted the Personnel Policy Manual Clarification Act to, again, reaffirm the existing Personnel Policy Manual, and to clarify that the Council is vested with the authority to approve any future modifications to the Manual and that the Governor is prohibited from making changes to the Manual by Executive Order; and

(c) The Governor and Council have identified the need to make improvements to the Personnel Policy Manual, and over the course of several years, proposed modifications to the Manual have been developed, circulated, and reviewed by multiple officials of the Pueblo.

New Section 6.7.1.100

Section 1.4. Approval

The Council hereby approves and enacts the attached Personnel Policy Manual.
