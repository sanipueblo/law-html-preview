Resolution No. SI-R25-007: First Amendment to the P.L. 93-638 Contract Authorization Act

At a duly called meeting of the Council of San Ildefonso Pueblo on the 16 day of October, 2025, the following resolution was adopted:

WHEREAS, In 2024, the Council enacted the P.L. 638-Contract Authorization Act ("Act") to authorize the Governor to execute Self-Determination Act contracts, also known as "638 Contracts" with the Bureau of Indian Affairs for the benefit of the Pueblo without the need for the Governor to seek repeated authorizations from the Council to execute a new 638 Contract or to renew an existing 638 Contract; and,

WHEREAS, The Council has identified the need to expand the scope of the Act to include the authorization of other 638 Contracts with the Indian Health Service and other federal agencies.

NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED, that the Council hereby enacts the attached resolution and law called, "The First Amendment to the P.L. 93-638 Contract Authorization Act".

CERTIFICATION

The foregoing resolution was duly voted upon by the Council on October 16, 2025, at a meeting with a vote of 8 in favor, 0 opposed, 1 abstaining and 1 absent (Governor presiding and not voting) pursuant to the authority vested in the Council by the Agreement of the Pueblo de San Ildefonso.

Raelene Martinez, Council Secretary

This enactment shall be known as the "The First Amendment to the P.L. 93-638 Contract Authorization Act" ("Act").

The purpose of this Act is to expand the scope of the Act to include the authorization of other 638 Contracts with the Indian Health Service and other federal agencies.

Section 1.4. Amendment of the Code

The Council hereby amends the Act as follows:

Part 6.5.39 — The P.L. 93-638 Contract Authorization Act

Amend Section 6.5.39.010

Section 6.5.39.010. Short Title

This enactment shall be known as the "THE P.L. 93-638 CONTRACT AUTHORIZATION ACT" ("Act").

Amend Section 6.5.39.020

Section 6.5.39.020. Purpose

The purpose of this Act to authorize the Governor to contract with the BIA, the Indian Health Service, and other federal agencies for programs and services that are beneficial to the Pueblo without the need to return to the Council for a resolution or authorization for the "638" contract, particularly for a renewal of an existing 638 Contract.

Amend Section 6.5.39.030

Section 6.5.39.030. Findings

The Council hereby finds and declares that:

(a) The Pueblo de San Ildefonso ("Pueblo") has contracted and administered various programs and services provided by the Bureau of Indian Affairs ("BIA"), the Indian Health Service ("HIS"), and other federal agencies through contracts pursuant to the Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act ("ISDEAA"), also known as P.L. 93-638 ("638 Contracts")

(b) The Pueblo is required to apply for initial "638 contracts" as well as for renewal of ongoing 638 Contracts.

(c) A Council resolution or authorization is required for the 638 Contract application and its submission to BIA, IHS, and other federal agencies.

(d) The Pueblo acknowledges the benefits received from the services provided by the various "638" contracted programs and wishes to continue to contract with BIA, IHS, and other federal agencies to receive funding to administer these programs.

(e) The Council has determined that it is in the best interest of the Pueblo to authorize the Governor to contract with the BIA, IHS, and other federal agencies for programs and services that are beneficial to the Pueblo without the need to continually return to the Council for a resolution or authorization for the "638" contract, particularly for a renewal of an existing contract.

(f) The Council has determined that is in the best interest of the Pueblo to authorize the Governor to negotiate and execute a contract agreement and to take any other actions necessary to effectuate a contract for the following BIA-contracted and IHS contracted programs including but not limited to:

  • Consolidated Tribal Government Program
  • Indian Child Welfare Act Program
  • Johnson O'Malley Program
  • Scholarships Program
  • Community Health Representatives
  • Alcohol and Substance Abuse, and
  • Any other IHS PFSA that is eligible to contract by the Pueblo in future contracts

(g) This Act, as amended, shall remain in effect until rescinded by the Council by law.

Amend Section 6.5.39.040

Section 6.5.39.040. Authorizations

(a) The Governor is authorized to contract with the BIA, IHS, and other federal agencies for programs and services that are beneficial to the Pueblo without the need to return to the Council for a resolution or authorization for the 638 Contract, particularly if it is for a renewal of an existing contract.

(b) The Governor is authorized to negotiate and execute a contract agreement and to take any other actions necessary to effectuate a contract for the following BIA-contracted, IHS-contracted, and other federal programs including but not limited to:

  • Consolidated Tribal Government Program
  • Indian Child Welfare Act Program
  • Johnson O'Malley Program
  • Scholarships Program
  • Community Health Representatives
  • Alcohol and Substance Abuse, and
  • Any other IHS PFSA that is eligible to contract by the Pueblo in future contracts

(c) The Governor is also authorized to negotiate and execute a new contract agreement for any other BIA, IHS, or other federal program not listed this Act, and upon execution of a 638 Contract, such program shall be subject to the continuing authorization of this Act.

(d) In the event that BIA, IHS, or other federal agency requires a specific resolution or other authorization for a 638 Contract, the Governor is authorized to issue a form of resolution or authorization in accordance with the Pueblo's Grant Authorization Act.

(e) When a 638 Contract application or agreement has been submitted or executed under the authorizations of this Act, the Governor shall notify the Council of such submission at the next Council meeting.

(f) This Act, as amended, shall remain in effect until rescinded by the Council by law.
